Media Composition | Sound Design | Audio Production


"Professional throughout" Richard Harding, Alternate Shadows Theatre

John is a freelance media composer based in the beautiful countryside of West Berkshire in England. He has experience working in audio branding, video games, theatre, writing to picture and audio drama. John also holds a Master's Degree in Professional Media Composition from the University of Chichester in the UK.

When you need dramatic audio that tells a story, then you need a dramatic composer!
