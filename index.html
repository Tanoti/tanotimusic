John Hicks Composer

Media Composition | Sound Design | Audio Production


I am a freelance media composer based in West Berkshire, England. I have worked on short films and audio dramas, written jingles and other music for Fantasy Conventions, and charities and created sound effects and backing tracks for theatrical productions.

Short Films and Projects

"The Gift" is a short film by Amy Rauch for her directing course, she asked me to write the music for it.

Kennet Amateur Theatrical Society asked me to mix a virtual choir recording of their members singing an arrangement of "Silent Night" by Siouxsie Millard for which I also composed the accompaniment.
