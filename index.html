John Hicks Composer

Media Composition | Sound Design | Audio Production


I am a freelance media composer based in West Berkshire, England. I have worked on short films and audio dramas, written jingles and other music for Fantasy Conventions and charities, and created sound effects and backing tracks for theatrical productions. I am currently studying for an MA in Professional Media Composition to improve my music writing, production and workflow skills.

Short Films and Projects

In 2023, Alternate Shadows Theatre Group asked if I could write some original music for their entry into the Kent Drama Association's Radio Play Festival. The play was "Wot Nots of My Dad" by Brian Walsh and, after reading the script, I offered to do the audio production work as well. I love audio drama and this was a great project to work on.

WE WON FIRST PLACE!

In 2021, the Kingsclere Players asked me to record and mix their radio play version of A Christmas Carol. This was a fun project where I got to write some original music and create the sound design. The recording was released to raise money for an ovarian cancer charity and hospice. Please have a listen and if you like it, consider making a donation to a cancer charity yourself.

"The Gift" is a short film by Amy Rauch for her directing course, she asked me to write the music for it.

Kennet Amateur Theatrical Society asked me to mix a virtual choir recording of their members singing an arrangement of "Silent Night" by Siouxsie Millard for which I also composed the accompaniment. This project provided the Society's members with a way to work together during the Covid-19 lockdown period, and I am proud to have been involved with this.
